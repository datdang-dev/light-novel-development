### 5. Archetype-Specific Speech Patterns

Different archetypes interact with lewd situations differently. DO NOT force every character to be a Brat.

#### B. The Shy / Passive (Submission > Fear/Pleasure)

#### C. The Matured / Onee-san (Teasing > Control)
