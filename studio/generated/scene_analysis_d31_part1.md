### 1. The Interrogation (The Mask)

### 2. The Yoko Deflection

* **Topic:** Yoko (Teacher) called Dat a "Perverted Pizza Man."
* **Asuka's Reaction:** Outrage on Dat's behalf. "She needs to be punished!"
* **Dat's Tactic:** Indifference. "The best punishment is to be ignored."
* **Psychology:** Asuka wants to be useful to him ("I'll assist you!"), trying to prove her loyalty by offering to attack his enemies. Dat rejects this to keep her insecure.

### 3. The "Special" Treatment & The Leverage

* **Asuka:** "Am I the *only* one you're giving the stick instead of the carrot?"
* **Dat:** "You're special."
* **The Turn:** Asuka tries to use her body/relationship as leverage.
  * *Attempt:* "Promise to only look at me when I'm around!"
  * *Dat's Counter:* "If you're going to paywall your party pouch, catch you later." (He threatens to walk away).
* **The Collapse:** Asuka instantly folds. The leverage fails because she needs him more than he needs her.

### 4. The "ChikTalk" Advice (Comedy/Tragedy)

* **Reveal:** Asuka admits she got dating advice from "ChikTalk" (TikTok parity) effectively telling her to "play hard to get."
* **Dat:** "Cunty Cosmo strikes again."
* **Impact:** Highlights Asuka's naivety and desperation. She is an elite student but socially deeply stunted regarding romance.
