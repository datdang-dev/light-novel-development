Asuka's route is a **Corruption/Domination** arc. It begins with mutual hostility, transitions into a power struggle where she is humiliated, and evolves into a twisted dependency where she finds safety in submission.

## üìÖ Timeline of Key Events

### **Phase 1: The High Horse (Days 1-20)**

* **Context:** Asuka is the untouchable Disciplinary Committee Chair. Dat is the "trash" transfer student.
* **Key Events:**
  * **First Encounter:** Asuka attempts to penalize Dat for minor infractions.
  * **The Hallway Clashes:** Repeated verbal sparring. Asuka insults Dat's poverty/status; Dat analyzes her insecurities.
  * **Dat's Planning:** Dat identifies her need for validation (from Helen) and her pride as her weak points.

### **Phase 2: The Humiliation (Day 21 - The Tennis Match)**

* **Event:** `label tennis_d21asukafuck`
* **Summary:** Asuka challenges Dat to a tennis match, expecting to crush him.
* **The Twist:** Dat initially throws the game to curry favor with Helen (Asuka's mom). Once Helen leaves, he reveals his true skill, completely dominating Asuka on the court.
* **The "Penalty" (Batsu Game):** Dat enforces a penalty game for her loss. He spanks her in public/semi-private context.
* **Impact:** Instead of being purely angry, Asuka is *aroused* by being overpowered and disciplined. This cracks her persona.

### **Phase 3: The Spiral (Days 22-30)**

* **Event:** `label asukadate026`
* **Summary:** Asuka becomes obsessed. She starts following Dat, driven by a confusing mix of hatred and lust.
* **Dynamics:**
  * She demanding he "take responsibility" for her confusing feelings.
  * Dat begins to categorize her based on her behavior: **Bitch** (Defiant), **Slave** (Submissive), or **Slut** (Lustful).
  * She begins interfering with other girls (Yoko, Minami) out of jealousy.

### **Phase 4: The Confession (Day 31 - The Restroom)**

* **Event:** `label d31_restroom_asuka_backstory`
* **Summary:** Asuka corners Dat in the restroom or follows him there.
* **The Reveal:** She confesses her childhood trauma‚ÄîHelen drove away her first crush, teaching her that "Men are pigs who must be controlled" or "Men will leave you."
