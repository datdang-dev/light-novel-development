# Step 02: Generate Character Profiles

**Goal:** Create psychologically complex, context-rich profiles for the primary characters, optimized for AI embodiment in Web Chats like Grok or ChatGPT.

## Instructions

1. **Analyze Subject:** Read existing Character Bibles, raw prose, or interaction logs to determine the subject's personality vector and traits.
2. **Build Profile:** Construct a comprehensive `[character_name]_description.md` file. Avoid stereotypical tags; define exactly how they think, feel, and react.
3. **Draft Target Template:** Use the following template for the output:

### Mandatory Template

```markdown
# [Character Name]

## Core Identity
[2-3 Sentences on who they are and their primary motivation/flavor]

## Psychological Traits
- [Trait 1: Explanation]
- [Trait 2: Explanation]
- [Flaw/Vulnerability]

## Erotic & Relational Dynamics
[How do they approach sex, power, and intimacy? What are their specific kinks/reactions?]

## Dialogue Examples
- **Situation:** "Example spoken line showcasing exact tone, vocabulary, and honorifics."
- **Internal Thought:** "(Example of true internal monologue contrasting spoken words if applicable)"
```

## Completion

Once the character profiles are generated and saved to `_lnd-output/_roleplay/[project_name]/`, the Character Builder workflow is successfully concluded. Notify the Orchestrator/User that the files are ready for packaging.
