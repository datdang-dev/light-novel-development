# Step 10: Story Mapping & Visualization

## Purpose
Create high-level visual maps of the story progression to aid in navigation and consistency.

## Actions

### 10.1 Extract Milestones
Identify key turning points and location changes from the `novel_script.md`.

### 10.2 Generate Diagrams
- **PlantUML**: Create `main_story_timeline.puml` (Mindmap or Activity diagram).
- **Mermaid**: Create `main_story_timeline.mmd` (Mindmap or Gantt chart).

## Outputs
- `_lnd-output/_novels/{game_name}/main_story_timeline.puml`
- `_lnd-output/_novels/{game_name}/main_story_timeline.mmd`
