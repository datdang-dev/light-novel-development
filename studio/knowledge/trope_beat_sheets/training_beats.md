# Training Beat Sheet (調教進行テンプレート)

> **Genre:** Training / 調教 (Chōkyō)
> **Core Appeal:** Systematic conditioning of a character through repeated encounters, each pushing boundaries further.
> **Key Rule:** Every session MUST escalate. Repetition without progression = failure.

---

## Mandatory Beat Progression

### Beat 1: CAPTURE/SETUP (捕獲)

- Target is placed in a controlled situation (abduction, blackmail, debt, contract, agreement)
- Trainer establishes RULES and CONSEQUENCES
- Target tests boundaries and fails
- Power dynamic is absolute: trainer has full control

### Beat 2: FIRST LESSON (初回調教)

- Introduction to basic acts (exposure, touching, basic oral)
- Target resists vigorously — punishment follows
- First forced pleasure: target orgasms against their will
- Shame and humiliation dominate the aftermath
- Trainer: "You came from this? Interesting."

### Beat 3: CONDITIONING (条件付け)

- Repeated sessions with Pavlovian reward/punishment structure
- Reward: pleasure, kindness, comfort when compliant
- Punishment: denial, pain, humiliation when defiant
- Target begins associating compliance with pleasure
- First voluntary action (even small: spreading legs, opening mouth)

### Beat 4: TESTING (試験)

- Trainer introduces a new challenge (public act, new partner, extreme position)
- Target must apply training without prompting
- Partial success: demonstrates conditioning is taking hold
- Failure points trigger correction, not punishment (trainer is "pleased" with effort)

### Beat 5: GRADUATION (卒業)

- Target performs without resistance or prompting
- Takes pride/satisfaction in pleasing the trainer
- Former disgust has become anticipation
- Stockholm syndrome / genuine attachment possible
- Target may request training ("Please teach me more")

---

## Session Structure (Per Training Encounter)

Each training session follows this mini-arc:

```
Instruction → Demonstration → Practice → 
Correction → Repetition → Reward
```

**Session Escalation Ladder:**

| Session | Focus | Intensity |
|---------|-------|-----------|
| 1 | Exposure, basic touch | Low — resistance high |
| 2 | Oral training (giving) | Medium — slight compliance |
| 3 | Oral + manual combined | Medium — body responding |
| 4 | First penetration | High — major resistance break |
| 5 | Advanced positions | High — compliance notable |
| 6 | Public/recorded acts | Maximum — identity shift |
| 7+ | Target-initiated acts | Complete conditioning |

## Trainer Dialogue Templates

| Phase | Pattern | Example |
|-------|---------|---------|
| Command | Direct, clinical | "Open your mouth. Wider." |
| Correction | Firm, disappointed | "Wrong. Again. Properly this time." |
| Praise | Measured, condescending | "Good girl. You're learning." |
| Testing | Challenging | "Do it yourself. Show me what I taught you." |
| Reward | Warm, possessive | "You've earned this. Such a good pet." |
