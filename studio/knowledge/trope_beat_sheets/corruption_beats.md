# Corruption Beat Sheet (堕落進行テンプレート)

> **Genre:** Corruption / 堕落 (Daraku) / 快楽堕ち (Kairaku-ochi)
> **Core Appeal:** Watching innocence transform into depravity through pleasure.
> **Key Rule:** Corruption is a ONE-WAY STREET. No instant recovery allowed.

---

## Mandatory Beat Progression

### Beat 1: INNOCENCE ESTABLISHED (純真)

- Character is genuinely innocent, naive, or morally upright
- Show their "normal" behavior: kindness, shyness, purity
- Establish what they find "unacceptable" (this will be broken)
- Gap Moe baseline is set here

### Beat 2: FIRST EXPOSURE (初体験)

- First encounter with sexual stimulus
- EXTREME discomfort and rejection
- Physical curiosity despite mental rejection
- "What is this feeling?" moment

### Beat 3: RELUCTANT PLEASURE (不本意な快楽)

- Second/third encounter — body begins to respond
- Mind says NO, body says YES
- Shame and confusion dominate
- First involuntary moan or physical reaction
- Key line pattern: "I don't want this... but why does it feel...?"

### Beat 4: RATIONALIZATION (合理化)

- Character begins making excuses for participation
- "It's just to gather information" / "I have no choice" / "One more time won't matter"
- The excuses grow thinner each time
- She starts doing things she previously said she'd never do

### Beat 5: CRAVING (渇望)

- The addiction takes hold
- Character initiates or seeks out the sexual encounter
- Previous moral values become obstacles to overcome, not boundaries to respect
- Body memory: physical reactions trigger without stimulus
- Dreams/fantasies about the acts

### Beat 6: CORRUPTION COMPLETE (堕落完了)

- Character fully embraces their new sexual identity
- Previous innocent self is referenced as contrast (Gap Moe payoff)
- New behaviors that would have horrified their former self
- Satisfaction/relief instead of shame
- Possible: she corrupts someone else (cycle continues)

## Corruption Indicators (Visible in Prose)

| Stage | Dialogue Pattern | Body Language | Internal Monologue |
|-------|-----------------|---------------|-------------------|
| Innocent | Polite, proper | Stiff, guarded | Clear, rational |
| Exposed | Stuttering, denial | Trembling, flinching | Confused, panicked |
| Reluctant | Begging to stop | Body relaxing against will | Contradictory ("Don't... but...") |
| Rationalizing | Making excuses | Subtly leaning in | Self-deceptive |
| Craving | Requesting more | Openly receptive | Honest desire |
| Corrupted | Dirty talk, demanding | Aggressive, shameless | Embracing depravity |
