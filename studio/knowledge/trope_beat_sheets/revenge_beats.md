# Revenge Beat Sheet (復讐進行テンプレート)

> **Genre:** Revenge / 復讐 (Fukushū)
> **Core Appeal:** Cathartic justice through sexual domination. The wronged party takes power back.
> **Key Rule:** The revenge must feel JUSTIFIED. Reader must root for the avenger, not pity the target.

---

## Mandatory Beat Progression

### Beat 1: THE WRONG (不正) — Establish the Crime

- Show the original injustice in vivid detail
- The victim suffers: humiliation, betrayal, loss, abuse
- Reader must feel genuine anger on behalf of the victim
- The perpetrator is shown to be arrogant, dismissive, or cruel
- Establish that normal justice is impossible or insufficient

### Beat 2: THE TURN (転機) — Power Shifts

- Victim gains the upper hand through:
  - Blackmail material (evidence, secrets, recordings)
  - Physical/social power shift (new status, strength, allies)
  - Opportunity (perpetrator is vulnerable, trapped, or alone)
- The moment the hunter becomes the hunted
- Perpetrator may not yet realize the shift

### Beat 3: FIRST RETRIBUTION (最初の報復) — Opening Move

- First sexual act of revenge
- Focus on the HUMILIATION of the perpetrator
- Mirror the original crime: make them experience what they inflicted
- Perpetrator's arrogance crumbles when they realize they're powerless
- Key satisfaction moment: "How does it feel?"

### Beat 4: ESCALATION (エスカレーション) — Deepening

- Each subsequent encounter increases in degradation
- Perpetrator is destroyed systematically:
  - Physical dignity (forced nudity, fluid exposure)
  - Social dignity (recordings, witnesses, public acts)
  - Mental dignity (made to beg, confess, degrade themselves verbally)
- Avenger may begin to enjoy the acts for their own sake (corruption of the avenger — optional complexity layer)

### Beat 5: BREAKING POINT (屈服) — Final Submission

- Perpetrator fully accepts their new position
- Options:
  - Complete submission (begging, devoted)
  - Broken defiance (still hates it but has no fight left)
  - Stockholm reversal (begins to crave the treatment)

### Beat 6: RESOLUTION (解決) — Justice Served

**Ending A — Clean Exit:** Avenger releases the perpetrator after satisfaction. Warning: "If you ever..."
**Ending B — Continued Domination:** The arrangement becomes permanent.
**Ending C — Role Reversal Complete:** Perpetrator becomes willing servant/slave.
**Ending D — Hollow Victory:** Avenger realizes revenge didn't heal. Bittersweet.

---

## Emotional Checkpoints

| Beat | Reader Emotion |
|------|---------------|
| 1 | Anger, sympathy for victim |
| 2 | Anticipation, satisfaction at power shift |
| 3 | Catharsis, "they deserved this" |
| 4 | Deepening satisfaction + moral complexity |
| 5 | Peak catharsis or discomfort (depending on tone) |
| 6 | Closure, satisfaction, or thoughtful ambiguity |
