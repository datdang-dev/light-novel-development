# Mind-Break Beat Sheet (精神崩壊進行テンプレート)

> **Genre:** Mind-Break / 精神崩壊 (Seishin Hōkai)
> **Core Appeal:** Watching a composed character's mind shatter from overwhelming pleasure.
> **Key Rule:** Mind-break is the CLIMAX of conquest. It must be EARNED through buildup.

---

## Mandatory Beat Progression

### Beat 1: COMPOSURE ESTABLISHED (冷静)

- Character demonstrates their "unbreakable" persona
- Show intelligence, willpower, or emotional control
- They believe they cannot be broken
- Gap Moe setup: the stronger the composure, the harder the fall

### Beat 2: FIRST ASSAULT (第一波)

- Overwhelming sexual stimulus introduced
- Character maintains composure through willpower
- Visible cracks: flushed face, gritted teeth, clenched fists
- Internal monologue: "I won't give in"
- Body begins betraying mind (wetness, hardening nipples, trembling)

### Beat 3: RESISTANCE STRAINING (抵抗の限界)

- Second wave of stimulus, more intense
- Composure slipping: moans escape, focus breaks
- Brief moments of ahegao that character "recovers" from
- Dialogue shifts: orders/defiance → bargaining/pleading
- Key pattern: "Stop... I can't... I won't lose to...!"

### Beat 4: THE BREAKING POINT (崩壊点)

**This is the central moment. MAXIMUM investment required.**

- A specific trigger overwhelms ALL remaining resistance
- The exact moment willpower shatters is described in EXTREME detail:
  - Eyes: Roll back, lose focus, go wide then glaze over
  - Mouth: Tongue lolls out, drool streams, lips can't close
  - Voice: Coherent speech → monosyllables → animal sounds
  - Body: Goes rigid → convulses → goes completely limp
- AHEGAO moment: the classic "broken face" expression in prose form
- Internal monologue stops mid-thought or becomes incoherent

### Beat 5: POST-BREAK STATE (崩壊後)

- Character is now a different person
- Previous personality traits are inverted:
  - Cold → desperately clingy
  - Proud → begging, submissive
  - Intelligent → single-minded pursuit of pleasure
  - Defiant → obedient, eager to please
- Physical markers: vacant eyes, constant drooling, twitching, hypersensitivity
- They cannot form complex thoughts or sentences

### Beat 6: NEW EQUILIBRIUM (新たな均衡)

- The broken state becomes the "new normal"
- Character may function normally in public but "switches" when triggered
- Physical conditioned responses: hearing the person's voice causes arousal
- Possible escalation: she seeks MORE extreme stimulus, baseline has shifted

## Anti-Patterns

- ❌ Instant mind-break without buildup (cheap, unsatisfying)
- ❌ Character recovers fully in next scene (invalidates the break)
- ❌ Mind-break from single normal orgasm (requires OVERWHELM, not just pleasure)
- ❌ Generic description ("She broke") — MUST show the exact physical process
