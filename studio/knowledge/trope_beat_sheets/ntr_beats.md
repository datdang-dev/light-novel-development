# NTR Beat Sheet (寝取られ進行テンプレート)

> **Genre:** NTR (寝取られ / Netorare)
> **Core Appeal:** Jealousy-driven arousal. The reader watches a beloved character be "stolen."
> **POV Options:** Victim perspective (cuckolded partner), Thief perspective (one doing the stealing), or Heroine perspective (being taken)

---

## Mandatory Beat Progression

### Beat 1: BELONGING (帰属) — Establish the Bond

**What:** Show the existing relationship as HAPPY and GENUINE.

- The couple must be visibly in love or at least attached
- Show intimate moments (not necessarily sexual) that prove the bond
- Reader must feel "these two belong together"

**Why:** NTR has ZERO impact if the reader doesn't first care about the bond being broken.

**Anti-Pattern:** ❌ Starting with an already unhappy relationship removes the taboo

---

### Beat 2: THE CRACK (亀裂) — Introduce the Threat

**What:** The third party appears and establishes their superiority.

- Physical superiority (more attractive, stronger, bigger)
- Social superiority (higher status, more money, more power)
- Sexual superiority (more experienced, more confident)
- The existing partner is shown to be lacking in contrast

**Key:** The threat must feel INEVITABLE. Reader should sense "this is going to happen."

---

### Beat 3: FIRST CONTACT (初接触) — The Line is Crossed

**What:** First inappropriate contact between heroine and the third party.

- Can be accidental or engineered
- Heroine MUST show resistance initially (even if brief)
- Internal conflict: loyalty vs attraction/fear/curiosity
- The existing partner is unaware

**Key:** The SECRECY is essential. The taboo doubles because it's hidden.

---

### Beat 4: DESCENT (陥落) — Resistance Crumbles

**What:** The heroine's resistance progressively weakens across encounters.

**Sub-beats:**
1. **Reluctant participation** ("Just this once...")
2. **Body betrayal** (Physical pleasure despite mental resistance)
3. **Comparison** (She starts comparing the two partners)
4. **Craving** (She initiates or doesn't resist anymore)

**This is the LONGEST beat.** Show the erosion gradually, not suddenly.

---

### Beat 5: REVELATION (発覚) — The Truth Surfaces

**What:** The original partner discovers (or suspects) the betrayal.

**Options:**
- Direct witness (walking in on them)
- Evidence discovery (photos, messages, marks on body)
- Behavior changes noticed (she flinches at his touch, smells different)
- Confession (she admits it, with or without remorse)

---

### Beat 6: AFTERMATH (結末) — Choose Your Ending

**Ending A — Corruption Complete:** Heroine fully chooses the new partner. Original partner is broken.
**Ending B — Regret Spiral:** Heroine regrets but can't stop. Cycle continues.
**Ending C — Reversal/Revenge:** Original partner takes action (recovery, revenge, NTL).

---

## Emotional Checkpoints (Reader Must Feel)

| Beat | Reader Emotion |
|------|---------------|
| 1 | Warmth, investment in the couple |
| 2 | Unease, dread, anticipation |
| 3 | Shock, arousal from the forbidden |
| 4 | Conflicted arousal + sympathy for victim |
| 5 | Devastation, catharsis |
| 6 | Depends on ending: despair / satisfaction / relief |
