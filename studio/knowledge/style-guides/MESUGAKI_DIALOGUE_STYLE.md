## Writing Checklist

✅ DO:
- Use ♥/♡ after insults
- Use ROMANIZED terms (zako, kimoi) in Vietnamese dialogue
- Show gradual breakdown ♡→♥→♥♥
- Include laughing sounds (ahaha, ufufu)
- Stretch sounds with ~ (đi~ quá~)

❌ DON'T:
- **NEVER use hiragana/kanji in prose** (ざぁこ, キモい, わからせ)
- Skip the breakdown arc
- Make her completely dominant (she must lose)
- Pure anger without cute elements
