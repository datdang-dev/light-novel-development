### 2.2 官能小説 (Erotic Novel) Techniques

From Japanese writing guides (官能小説の書き方):

#### Core Principles
1. **心理描写** (Psychological Description)
   - Show internal thoughts, not just actions
   - Build to moment - reader needs "納得感" (sense of justification)

2. **五感描写** (Five Senses)
   - 視覚 (Visual): Body, fluids, expressions
   - 聴覚 (Auditory): Moans, wet sounds, breathing
   - 嗅覚 (Olfactory): Sweat, arousal, aftermath
   - 触覚 (Tactile): Temperature, texture, pressure
   - 味覚 (Taste): Kisses, oral, fluids

3. **事後描写** (Aftermath Description)
   - NEVER skip post-climax
   - Show physical residue, exhaustion, emotional state
   - Male caring for female = reader satisfaction

4. **「官能的」vs「性的」** (Sensual vs Sexual)
   - Can create arousal WITHOUT explicit acts
   - Gestures, clothing, atmosphere, suggestion
   - Professional writers master this boundary

## 4. Character Archetypes (キャラ属性)

### 4.1 Female Archetypes

#### Mesugaki (メスガキ)
> See: [MESUGAKI_DIALOGUE_STYLE.md](./MESUGAKI_DIALOGUE_STYLE.md)

- **Core:** Bratty, provocative, looks down on adults
- **Speech:** "ざぁこ♡", mock baby-talk
- **Arc:** Confident mockery → overwhelmed → broken submission
- **Appeal:** "Brat correction" fantasy

#### Gyaru (ギャル)
- **Core:** Rebellious, fashion-forward, free-spirited
- **Appearance:** Dyed hair, makeup, accessories, tanned
- **Subtypes:** 
  - Hime Gyaru (princess-like)
  - Agejo Gyaru (nightlife, glamorous)
- **Speech:** Casual, slang-heavy, "~っしょ"
- **Appeal:** Perceived promiscuity, confidence

#### Kuudere (クーデレ)
- **Core:** Calm, stoic, emotionless exterior
- **Etymology:** クール (cool) + デレデレ (lovestruck)
- **Arc:** Cold → cracks appear → genuine affection emerges
- **Speech:** Minimal, terse, "...そう"
- **Sex behavior:** Silent trembling, rare emotional breaks

#### Yandere (ヤンデレ)
- **Core:** Sweet exterior, obsessive interior
- **Etymology:** 病んでる (yanderu, sick) + デレデレ
- **Trigger:** Jealousy → violence
- **Appeal:** Dangerous devotion, possession

#### MILF/Hitozuma (人妻)
- **Core:** Mature, often maternal figure
- **Appeal:** Experience, forbidden/taboo
- **Common scenarios:** Step-family, neighbor, friend's mom
- **Speech:** "あらあら~", gentle but knowing

## 7. Dialogue Format Standards

### 7.1 Bracket Conventions

| Symbol | Usage |
|--------|-------|
| 「」 | Standard dialogue |
| 『』 | Inner thoughts, quotes |
| （）| Whispers, asides |

### 7.2 Tag-less Dialogue

In fast-paced scenes, omit speaker tags:
```
「待って...」
「何が？」
「早すぎ...っ」
「まだまだ」
```
