# Dat (Player) - Character Profile

## üß† Core Psychology

Dat is a classic "Avenger" protagonist driven by a deep-seated hatred for the Sazaki family and the town that destroyed his father. His worldview is transactional and cynical; he views people as "tools," "obstacles," or "targets" to be used for his revenge or pleasure.

However, he is also an **Unreliable Narrator** and a **Hypocrite**.

1. **Justification:** He constantly justifies his immoral actions (blackmail, drugging, manipulation) as "karmic justice" or "necessary for survival," claiming moral superiority over his victims even while doing worse things to them.
2. **The "Saint" Mask:** externally, he plays the role of the polite, clumsy, well-meaning transfer student to gain trust.
3. **Lust vs. Mission:** While he claims to be focused on revenge, he is easily distracted by his high libido ("Waifu Academy"), often jeopardizing or pausing his plans to pursue sexual conquests.
