## Key Traits to Copy:
1. **Messy & Continuous:** The dialogue flows continuously, mixing thoughts, emotes (`<3`, `:3`), and breaking the 4th wall natively ("có ai honggg cíu tui với").
2. **Action = Stage Directions:** Action lines are brutally simple and flat ("lão thầy giáo lao đến đè cô ấy... ông ta lôi cặc ra bắt đầu đụ tới tấp"). No poetic description of the air, temperature, or dramatic feelings.
3. **Pacing:** Shifts from taunting -> fake resistance ("em hong mún có thai đâu") -> taunting again when the dominant figure loses.
