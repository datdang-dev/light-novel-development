### The "Chain Reaction" Protocol (V6.1)

```text
Every piece of dialogue/SFX MUST be anchored by a 3-part structure:
1. Pre-speech (Physical Cause/Friction/Smell)
2. The Dialogue (The Anchor)
3. Post-speech (Bodily Response/Residue/Expression)

### Banned Words (AUTO-FAIL)

```text

### 2. Write the Dialogue-Driven Prose

Construct the prose paragraph by paragraph. **Do not pre-write dry actions.** Instead, follow the chronological dialogue sequence.

```markdown

**MANDATORY PRE-REQUISITE (COT SCRATCHPAD):**
Before writing the actual prose for the scene beat, you MUST output a `<think>` block applying the Deep Forensic Framework and verifying the active Modules.

```xml

For each parsed dialogue/SFX:

1. **Pre-speech (Sensory build-up):**
   Describe the physical action or smell that CAUSES the character to speak. 
   - What is rubbing?
   - What fluid is leaking?
   - What sensation is forcing their mouth open?

2. **The Output (Format Strict):**
   Inject the dialogue exactly as transcribed (translated/adapted).
   `Character: 「Speech Content」`

3. **Post-speech (Psychological & Bodily Degradation):**
   Immediately follow up with the body's betrayal.
   - Drool dripping from the mouth.
   - Vaginal walls convulsing.
   - Eyes rolling back / haze settling in.
   - The contrast between haughty words and a pathetic physical state.
```

**GOONER REQUIREMENTS (MANDATORY):**

- **Lexicon Injection:** Use minimum 2 words from `hentai_lexicon.md` per block (e.g., nhớp nháp, bựa cặc, xục xạo).
- **X-Ray View:** If penetration occurs, describe the internal walls gripping or resisting.
- **Fluid Worship:** If fluids are present (smegma, arousal fluid), explicitly mention the taste, smell, or texture bridging the characters.
- **No Euphemisms:** Explicit anatomy only.
